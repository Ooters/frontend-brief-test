Things I Made

    a slow rss reader that only checks feeds once a day, on purpose. for people who don’t want to know everything immediately.

  • BREWING marginalia.css

    a tiny css library for laying out books on the web with proper margins, drop caps, and footnotes that don’t embarrass themselves.

    send one short email to one person, once. no thread, no reply-all, no subject line. closed beta because honestly i’m not sure anyone wants this.

    an ambitious browser-based collage tool. i learned an enormous amount and shipped exactly nothing. the source is still up.
